Reformer vs Mat Pilates: what should you choose in Mallorca?

Choose by goal, budget, and preferred coaching format. Use this page to avoid random trial-and-error.

Reformer Pilates

  • Adjustable resistance and progressive loading.
  • Useful for strength, posture, and low-impact conditioning.
  • Typically higher class cost due to equipment.

Mat Pilates

  • Accessible and usually lower cost.
  • Excellent for core control and mobility fundamentals.
  • Can be combined with home practice between classes.
Decision shortcut: If you want faster guided progression and budget is flexible, start with Reformer. If you want a lower-cost entry point, start with mat and layer in Reformer later.
