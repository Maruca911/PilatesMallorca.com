Private vs group Reformer in Mallorca

Pick the format that matches your budget, confidence level, and desired speed of progress.

Private sessions

  • Maximum personalization and cue precision.
  • Best for injury-aware modifications and fast feedback loops.
  • Higher price per session.

Group classes

  • Lower cost per class and social motivation.
  • Great for consistency and habit formation.
  • Less individualized cueing per minute.

Best-fit scenarios

  • Start private if you are new, returning after pain, or preparing for a specific event.
  • Choose group if your basics are stable and you want affordable frequency.
  • Hybrid model: 1 private session each month + group classes weekly.
