Reformer vs Barre vs Yoga: Mallorca decision guide

Each method solves a different problem. This page helps you pick quickly.

Reformer

Best for guided resistance, posture, and low-impact strength.

Barre

Best for muscular endurance and high-rep conditioning.

Yoga

Best for mobility, breathwork, and stress reduction.

Most clients benefit from a mixed week: 2 Reformer sessions + 1 Yoga or Barre session.
