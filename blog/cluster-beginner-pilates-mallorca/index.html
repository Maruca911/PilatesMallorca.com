Beginner FAQ

Should I start with mat or Reformer?

Both can work. If you want more guidance and controlled loading, Reformer can be a strong start. If you want low-cost consistency, mat can be a great base. Many people combine both.

What should I bring to a Reformer class?

Comfortable fitted clothing and (often) grip socks. Check the studio policy before you go.

How quickly can you send recommendations?
