Athlete quick FAQ

Should athletes do mat Pilates or Reformer?

Both can work. Reformer can help with guided loading and feedback; mat can build control with minimal equipment. Choose based on constraints, coaching access, and training load.

Can Pilates replace strength training?

It depends on your sport and goals. Many athletes use Pilates to improve control and movement quality, then pair it with strength training for higher-force development.
