Private sessions FAQ

Should I book private Reformer or private mat Pilates?

If you want guided strength progression and more support for positions, Reformer can be a strong option. If you want a travel-friendly baseline, mat can be great. Tell us your goal and we will shortlist the best fit.

How do you match me with the right teacher?

We match by goal, level, constraints, language, and location, then shortlist 3 options that fit availability.

Do you include retreats and studios too?

Yes. You can request a teacher-only shortlist, or ask for a mix of teachers, studios, and retreats.
