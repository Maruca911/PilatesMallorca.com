What to prioritize

  • Instructor experience with prenatal/postnatal modifications.

  • Comfort-first movement choices (no pain chasing).

Prenatal/Postnatal quick FAQ

Should I choose private sessions during pregnancy?

If you want the most control and individualized modifications, private sessions can be a strong option. Group classes can work too if they are truly beginner-friendly and the teacher can scale exercises.

Can you match providers by language?

Yes. English, German, Spanish, and Swedish preferences are supported in the form.

What is the fastest next step?

Submit the match form and include notes about your stage and constraints so we can shortlist suitable options.
