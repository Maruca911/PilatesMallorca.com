Beginner questions

Can I start Reformer Pilates with no experience?

Yes. Choose beginner-friendly classes and tell us your experience level in the match form.

How many classes per week should beginners do?

Most beginners progress well with 2 sessions/week for the first month.

What should I ask before booking my first studio?

Ask about onboarding process, class size, instructor experience, and language options.

Can I combine mat Pilates and Reformer?

Yes. A common approach is 2 Reformer classes plus 1 mat session per week.

Where can I get beginner tutorials first?

See beginner video guide and starter checklist.

Pricing and plans

How much does a group Reformer class cost in Mallorca?

Often around EUR 20-35 per class, depending on area and studio profile.

How much is a private Pilates teacher in Mallorca?

Private sessions are usually higher than group sessions and vary by location and coach profile.

Are package plans cheaper than single class passes?

Usually yes. Many studios offer better value with 8-12 class bundles.

Can I get both low-cost and premium options?

Yes. We can send a mixed shortlist matching your budget range.

Is your matching service free?

Yes. The user matching flow is free.
